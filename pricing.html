Yes! Early access shops get 1 month completely free with no credit card required. After that, you can continue with any paid plan or cancel anytime.

Our AI is trained on 100,000+ real repair cases with 500+ symptoms, 250+ causes, and 150+ diagnostic tests. It includes panic log decoding, IMEI lookup, and safety detection.

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. If you upgrade, you'll be prorated. If you downgrade, the new rate starts at your next billing cycle.

All major tech devices: iPhones, Samsung, Pixel, game consoles (Xbox, PlayStation, Switch, Steam Deck), MacBooks, laptops, smartwatches, AirPods, and more.
How does the AI auto-reply work?+
Connect your SMS, Instagram, Facebook Messenger, or phone line. Our AI uses the same diagnostic engine to automatically respond to customer inquiries 24/7.
