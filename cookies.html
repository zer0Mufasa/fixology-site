Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 4, 2024

This Cookie Policy explains how Fixology ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website at fixologyai.com.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for basic site functionality like login and navigation Session
Functional Remember your preferences and settings 1 year
Analytics Understand how visitors use our site (anonymized) 2 years

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They include:

  • Authentication cookies for logged-in users
  • Security cookies to prevent fraud
  • Load balancing cookies for performance

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization:

  • Language and region preferences
  • Theme settings (dark/light mode)
  • Recently viewed items

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve the user experience. All analytics data is anonymized.

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies already on your computer and set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust preferences every time you visit our site.

Third-Party Cookies

We do not allow third-party advertising cookies. Any third-party services we use (like payment processors) have their own cookie policies.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at privacy@fixologyai.com.
